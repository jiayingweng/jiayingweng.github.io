I am an Assistant Professor of Mathematical Sciences at Bentley University. Prior to joining Bentley, I received my Ph.D. in Statistics from the University of Kentucky under the supervision of Dr. Xiangrong Yin.

Research

My primary research interests encompass sufficient dimension reduction, variable selection, and high-dimensional data analysis. Sufficient dimension reduction is a statistical technique that aims to find a low-dimensional representation of the predictor variables while preserving the information about the response variable.
